Marco Fiege brings a wealth of expertise in global logistics and supply chain management, with a particular focus on the critical trade corridors connecting Europe, the Middle East, and Central Asia. With a background in one of Europe's leading logistics families, he has deep operational knowledge of freight management, warehousing, and distribution networks.

Fiege has been instrumental in developing logistics solutions for the Middle Corridor and Trans-Caspian routes, which are central to the GCR's strategic significance. His network of logistics operators, port authorities, and government regulators across the region provides GCR clients with unmatched infrastructure access.

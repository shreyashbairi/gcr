Strategic Landscape

In the Greater Caspian Region (GCR), customs and certification are the main tools for protecting the internal market. Especially for equipment suppliers in Oil, Gas, and Critical Minerals spheres, where the cost of border downtime is measured in millions, regulatory precision becomes business-critical. Our algorithms simplify the passage of critical stages:

  • Seamless Homologation We perform professional product adaptation to local standards (GOST/TechReg), ensuring direct market access. We synchronize your specifications with regulator requirements, guaranteeing sales legality from day one.
  • Secure Tech Transfer We possess deep expertise in classification and export control, creating conditions for stable technology supplies. We ensure operational purity, allowing business development even in sensitive sectors safely.
  • Green Channel Operations We provide absolute precision in documentation and labeling, opening the possibility of accelerated customs clearance. Flawless cargo processing accelerates capital turnover and logistic flows.

We turn the "License to Operate" acquisition process into a predictable algorithm, ensuring your cargo "Green Corridor" status.

What We Deliver

Core Capabilities

01. Product Homologation & Certification

We take on the entire legalization cycle. We interact with accredited laboratories and state bodies to adapt your specifications to local technical regulations. We confirm product safety and conformity before the first pallet leaves your warehouse.

02. Global Trade Compliance (WTO & Sanctions Shield)

We ensure the purity of export-import operations. Our experts conduct a deep analysis of HS Codes to optimize customs duties and check deals for compliance with sanctions restrictions. We create a protective perimeter eliminating risks of account or cargo blocking.

03. Business Registration & Tax ID

Obtaining necessary tax and statistical identifiers for conducting trading activities. We synchronize this process with certification so that by the moment of goods importation, you have a full package of documents for customs clearance.

Comparison of your product specifications with local norms. Identification of refinement needs (labeling, instruction, composition).

Phase 2: Homologation

Laboratory tests and product adaptation.

Phase 3: Submission

Dossier submission to the regulator through accelerated channels.

Phase 4: Clearance

Obtaining certificates and successful customs passage.

Project Spotlight

Client

Manufacturer of drilling reagents (Oil & Gas Chemicals)

Context

The client faced a refusal to import a batch at the customs of one of the Central Asian countries due to the absence of a specific sanitary-epidemiological conclusion, which was not required in the EU.

Solution

Our Regulatory Shield team urgently joined the process. We classified the product according to local codes, organized accelerated laboratory testing of samples on-site, and obtained the conclusion in 5 working days (instead of the standard 30).

Result

Cargo cleared without downtime fines. A preliminary labeling check protocol was implemented, preventing incident recurrence.

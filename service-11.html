Business Services in GCR and Worldwide

Corporate Governance, Integrity & Fiduciary Shield

We protect foreign shareholders from fraud, misappropriation, and governance failures - and build the systems that prevent them from recurring.

Strategic Landscape

When a foreign-owned business operates in the GCR, the foreign shareholder is rarely in the room. That distance creates space for problems to develop quietly - and in this region, they often do.

The issues we encounter most often are not dramatic. A local director with undisclosed interests in a competing business. Procurement consistently directed to suppliers connected to management. A customer list being moved to a new entity being built by the same team. By the time any of this reaches the shareholder's desk, the damage is done - and the longer it ran, the more expensive it is to fix.

Who it's for

Foreign shareholders and HQ legal teams with concerns about the integrity of GCR operations, or seeking to establish robust governance proactively

When you need it

You suspect fraud, asset misappropriation, IP leakage, or undisclosed conflicts of interest; or you are installing new management and need to verify the actual state of the business

What you get

Independent investigation with findings and evidence + identified threats removed + governance framework established + IP protected + management replaced if required

Timeline

Initial diagnostic - 2-3 weeks; full investigation and remediation - 6-12+ weeks depending on complexity

What We Deliver

Core - Investigation & Protection

01. Business Health Check & Investigations

We assess what is actually happening in your GCR operation - not what the accounts show, but what is going on in procurement, cash flow, related-party relationships, and management behaviour. Where findings warrant it, we escalate to a formal investigation with evidence structured for legal proceedings.

02. Anti-Corruption & Compliance Framework

We implement the internal controls and reporting procedures required under the US Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA) and the UK Bribery Act - protecting your business from liability and ensuring you are ready for the compliance review that comes with any acquisition or investment.

03. IP & Technology Protection

We register your trademarks, proprietary technology, and trade secrets in the jurisdictions where you operate, and establish the contractual boundaries that prevent unauthorised use. Where IP has already been transferred without authorisation, we manage enforcement.

04. Dawn Raid Readiness

We prepare your local team for a regulatory inspection or law enforcement visit - what they must do, what they are not obliged to permit, and how to protect privileged materials while remaining fully cooperative. Most companies think about this only after it has happened.

Ongoing Governance (LTV Options)

Board Advisory & PMO

Independent advisory presence at board level. For complex operational change programs, a Project Management Office function to track delivery and ensure decisions made at the top are executed at ground level.

Executive Search

Where investigation findings require management changes, we manage the search and selection of replacement leadership - from local market knowledge, with full background verification.

The Methodology

Diagnostic

Initial business health check. Highest-risk areas identified, determination of whether deeper investigation is warranted.

Investigation

Formal investigation where findings justify it. Evidence gathered and documented to the standard required for legal or disciplinary proceedings.

Remediation

Threats removed. Management changes made if required. IP registered and protected. Compliance framework implemented.

Governance Activation

Ongoing oversight structures established. Board advisory engaged. PMO activated for any material change program.

Business Services in GCR and Worldwide

Digital Ecosystems & Innovation Sourcing

We get your products selling on the platforms that dominate regional commerce - and find local technology solutions that work where Western vendors don't.

Strategic Landscape

Digital commerce in the GCR does not run on the same platforms or by the same rules as Western markets. Kaspi in Kazakhstan, Uzum in Uzbekistan, and Wildberries each have their own search algorithms, promotional mechanics, logistics requirements, and seller compliance standards. Simply listing a product achieves nothing - performance is determined by how well a seller understands and works within each platform's specific ecosystem.

The same applies to technology. Many international software solutions are unavailable in the region, too expensive relative to local alternatives, or poorly adapted to local payment infrastructure, language, and regulatory requirements. In several sectors, locally developed technology has outpaced international equivalents in both functionality and market penetration. Companies that treat GCR digital markets as a straightforward extension of their existing strategy consistently underperform.

Who it's for

CDOs, e-commerce directors, and R&D leads expanding into GCR digital channels or sourcing local technology

When you need it

Western digital tools are unavailable or uncompetitive in the region; you need to launch on local platforms; or you are looking for local technology alternatives to expensive international vendors

What you get

Active, optimized product listings on target platforms + verified local technology partners + R&D processes adapted to the pace of regional markets

Timeline

Platform activation - 4-8+ weeks; technology scouting and verification - 6-10+ weeks depending on the situation

What We Deliver

Core - Digital Market Entry

01. E-Commerce Platform Entry

Entry onto dominant regional platforms - Kaspi, Uzum, Wildberries, and others relevant to your category. Product catalogue configured for each platform's search and ranking algorithm, promotional programs set up, seller terms negotiated, and last-mile logistics established to meet platform delivery requirements. Each platform treated as a distinct commercial channel - because that is what it is.

02. Technology Scouting & Partner Verification

Local and regional technology companies identified whose solutions are relevant to your operational or product needs - in payments, logistics software, data analytics, industrial automation, or other sectors. Verified thoroughly: financial stability, actual product capability, client references, team reliability. Output: a shortlist of partners we are prepared to recommend, not a directory of names.

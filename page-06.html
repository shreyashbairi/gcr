Is your business ready for expansion into the Greater Caspian Region?

The Greater Caspian Region (GCR) is one of the fastest-growing markets, which often remains a "blind spot" for Western business. This is a colossal potential: over 640 million people, a GDP of over $8.4 trillion (PPP), and the status of the world's main logistical hub. But are you ready to work in a region where success depends not only on the product, but also on an understanding of the local context?

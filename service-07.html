Business Services in GCR and Worldwide

Industrial Footprint & Supply Chain Architecture

We get your facility built, your logistics running, and your supply chain operational - on time, within budget, without surprises.

Strategic Landscape

Industrial projects in the GCR fail at execution far more often than at planning. Infrastructure that looks adequate on paper is sometimes unavailable in practice. Logistics partners agree to service levels they cannot sustain. Customs procedures create delays no one budgeted for. Procurement costs diverge from projections because local sourcing dynamics were not mapped before commitments were made.

These are not exceptional risks - they are the standard operating environment. The difference between a project that launches on schedule and one that runs six months late almost always comes down to whether someone with direct operational knowledge in the region was involved in setup from the beginning.

Who it's for

COOs, Supply Chain Directors, and industrial investors setting up manufacturing, warehousing, or logistics operations in the GCR

When you need it

You need to select a site, establish a working facility, and build a supply chain - and cannot afford delays or structural errors in an unfamiliar environment

What you get

Verified site + functioning logistics network with enforceable service levels + customs clearance managed + procurement costs optimized

Timeline

Site selection and technical validation - 4-8 weeks; full operational setup is project-dependent

What We Deliver

Phase 1 - Site & Infrastructure (Core)

01. Site Selection & Technical Due Diligence

Candidate sites assessed against your operational requirements - not just location and cost, but actual infrastructure availability: power capacity, gas, water, road access, proximity to logistics corridors. We verify what is real, not what is stated in industrial zone promotional materials. Available government incentives tied to specific sites are identified and factored into the comparison.

02. Logistics Partner Sourcing & Contracting

Identification, evaluation, and contracting of third-party logistics providers - assessed on actual capacity, equipment condition, track record, and financial stability. We negotiate service level agreements that are realistic and enforceable, and stay involved through activation to ensure delivery matches commitment.

03. Customs & Cross-Border Management

Customs clearance for equipment imports, raw material flows, and finished goods exports. Tariff classification, documentation preparation, and coordination with customs authorities. For regular freight flows, we work to establish priority clearance arrangements that reduce processing time materially.

Phase 2 - Operational Systems (LTV Options)

S&OP Implementation (Sales & Operations Planning)

Production planning aligned with commercial demand - reducing inventory buildup, preventing stockouts, providing leadership with a single reliable view of operational performance.

Strategic Sourcing & Procurement Optimization

Procurement spend analyzed, local sourcing options identified, supplier contracts renegotiated to reduce cost of goods and reduce single-source dependency.

The Methodology

Site & Feasibility

Technical due diligence, infrastructure verification, incentive mapping, final site recommendation.

Build & Contract

Facility setup finalized. Logistics partners selected, contracted, SLAs agreed.

Customs & Activation

Customs framework established. Equipment and materials cleared. First operational flows activated.

Optimization

S&OP implemented. Procurement renegotiated. Operational performance stabilized and benchmarked.

Privacy Policy & Data Protection Statement

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

1. Introduction and Controller

Swiss Project Development Company SA ("Company", "we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you visit our website or use our "Readiness Check" services, in compliance with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data Controller:
Swiss Project Development Company SA

Representative:

Contact for Privacy Matters:

2. Data We Collect

We collect data in two ways: automatically when you visit the site, and directly when you provide it to us.

2.1. Technical Data (Automated)

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address (automatically anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Time zone setting and location (Country/City)
  • Operating system and platform
  • Usage data (how you interact with our website)

2.2. "Readiness Check" Data (Voluntarily Provided)

To generate your customized consulting report/graph, we ask you to answer approximately 10 specific questions about your business.

  • Categories of Data: This includes information regarding your business strategy, operational status, financial indicators (revenue, profit margins), tax situations, and personnel/HR matters.
  • Sensitivity: We treat this information as highly confidential and sensitive business profiling data.
  • Anonymity Phase: You answer these questions anonymously. Your identity (Email/Name) is linked to these answers only when you choose to submit your contact details to receive the final report.

2.3. Contact Data

  • Name, Email address, Phone number (if provided).

3. Purposes of Processing

We use your data for the following specific purposes:

  • To Provide the Readiness Check: Processing your answers to generate the automated consulting graph/report.
  • Communication: Sending you the report, invoice, or follow-up consulting proposals based on your results.
  • Analytics: Improving our website and services using aggregated, non-identifiable data.
  • Marketing: Retargeting and sending newsletters (only with your explicit consent).

4. Automated Decision Making and Profiling

Our "Readiness Check" service uses automated processing to analyze your answers and generate a visual graph/report of your business status.

  • Logic Involved: The algorithm scores your inputs on financial, HR, and strategic metrics to benchmark your "readiness."
  • Your Rights: If you disagree with the automated assessment, you have the right to request a manual review by one of our consultants.

5. Data Sharing and International Transfers

We do not sell your data. However, we use trusted third-party service providers ("Processors") to operate our business. Some data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of Switzerland/EU, specifically the USA.

Service Providers:

  • Shopify (Canada/USA): For website hosting and content management.
  • Google & Meta (USA): For analytics and marketing.

Transfer to the USA:
For providers based in the USA (e.g., Google, Meta, Shopify), we rely on the Swiss-US Data Privacy Framework and/or Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure your data is protected at a level equivalent to Swiss law.

6. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for.

Readiness Check Data: Retained for 3 years after submission.

  • If you provided contact details: Data is linked to your profile to offer you benchmark comparisons if you return in the future.
  • If submitted anonymously: Data is retained only in an aggregated, non-identifiable format for statistical analysis.
  • Contact Information: Retained until you request deletion or unsubscribe.
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 14 months (Google Analytics standard).

7. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way.

  • All data transferred between your browser and our site is encrypted via SSL/TLS.
  • Access to sensitive "Readiness Check" answers is restricted to authorized personnel and consultants only.

8. Your Legal Rights

Under the nFADP and GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Ask us to delete your data (e.g., your Readiness Check answers) at any time.
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your data for marketing purposes.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request the transfer of your data to you or a third party.

How to exercise these rights:
Please contact us at info@tethys-gcr.com. We will respond to legitimate requests within 30 days.

9. Cookies

We use cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the site to function (Shopify).
  • Marketing/Analytics Cookies: Used by Google and Meta. You can opt-out of these via our Cookie Banner or your browser settings.

Swiss Project Development Company SA reserves the right to update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated via email or a notice on our website.

Terry Granville is an internationally recognized expert in trade promotion and foreign direct investment. With a distinguished career spanning government advisory roles and private sector consulting, he has helped shape trade policy frameworks and investment strategies across Europe, the Middle East, and Central Asia.

Granville has served as a trade envoy and advisor to multiple government bodies, building extensive networks with trade ministers, investment authorities, and multilateral organizations. His expertise in navigating regulatory landscapes and facilitating cross-border investment makes him instrumental to GCR's ability to open doors in complex jurisdictions.

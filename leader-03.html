Terry Granville is an internationally recognized expert in trade promotion and foreign direct investment. Granville has served as a trade envoy and advisor to multiple government bodies, building extensive networks with trade ministers, investment authorities, and multilateral organizations. His expertise in navigating regulatory landscapes and facilitating cross-border investment makes him instrumental to GCR's ability to open doors in complex jurisdictions.

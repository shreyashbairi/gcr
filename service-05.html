Negotiation Mastery & Channel Performance

We unblock stalled deals and turn opaque distribution networks into transparent, accountable revenue operations.

Strategic Landscape

In the GCR, a signed contract is the beginning of the process, not the end. Execution depends on personal relationships and a level of trust that legal instruments alone cannot create or enforce. When a distributor underperforms, the stated reason is almost never the real one. When a negotiation stalls, applying more corporate pressure typically makes it worse.

Both situations are solvable - but not through standard commercial channels. They require someone who understands the actual dynamics at play and knows how to move things forward without creating a public conflict that damages the relationship permanently.

  • Who it's forVPs of Sales, Commercial Directors, and M&A teams dealing with underperforming partners or deadlocked negotiations in the GCR
  • When you need itA distributor is missing targets or withholding data; a negotiation has stalled; a partner relationship has turned adversarial
  • What you getUnblocked negotiations + root cause diagnosis + structured channel management system + measurable improvement in sales performance
  • TimelineInitial diagnosis - 3-4 weeks; channel restructuring and first results - typically within 3-4+ months depending on the situation

What We Deliver

Core - Deal & Network Architecture

01. Shadow Negotiations & Cultural Mediation

We enter at critical moments in a negotiation or deteriorating partner relationship. Working behind the scenes - not as your public representative - we identify the real obstruction, find the path to resolution, and move the situation toward a conclusion on your terms. The relationship is preserved. The conflict is resolved without escalation.

02. Partner Network Architecture

We restructure your distribution channel into a managed commercial system: formal performance standards, accountability mechanisms, and a reporting structure that gives you genuine visibility into what your partners are actually doing. The goal is not bureaucracy - it is making partner performance measurable so you stop managing by intuition.

Sustainable Revenue Engine (LTV Options)

Sales Rituals & Commercial Discipline

Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs), pipeline reporting, and CRM accountability integrated into partner relationships. A regional Sales Playbook so performance depends on the system, not on individuals.

After-Sales & Service Strategy

A service model - warranty management, spare parts, technical training - that turns service costs into a revenue stream and protects your brand where most damage happens: after the sale.

The Methodology

Phase 1: Cultural Alignment

Situation diagnosis. Shadow negotiations to surface the real issue and align expectations.

Phase 2: Network Structuring

Partner performance standards, accountability mechanisms, and management reporting implemented.

Phase 3: Engine Ignition

QBRs, pipeline reviews, partner training, and CRM integration launched.

Phase 4: Value Extraction

After-sales service designed. Partner performance and customer lifetime value optimized.

Project Spotlight

Client

Mining Equipment Manufacturer

Context

Exclusive distributor in Kazakhstan showed a 40% drop in sales, citing a "hard market," while refusing to provide detailed customer reports.

Solution

We conducted Shadow Negotiations with the distributor's owner, finding out the true reason (conflict of interest with another brand). We revised the contract, implemented the Distributor Excellence Program with mandatory monthly QBRs, and introduced our representative into their commercial council.

Result

Pipeline transparency restored in 2 months. Sales grew by 25% in the first quarter after implementing Sales Rituals. Relationships transferred from "toxic" to constructive.
